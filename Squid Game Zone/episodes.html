Episodes

Episode 1: Red Light, Green Light

In the first episode, a group of strangers are invited to play a deadly game for a huge cash prize. They face a twisted version of “Red Light, Green Light,” where losing means death. The game quickly reveals the deadly stakes.

Episode 2: Hell

After the first game, the players are given the option to leave, but many decide to go back. Life outside is just as hard, and some end up returning to the game, knowing it’s dangerous but with no other choice.

Episode 3: The Man with the Umbrella

The players face the next round, where they must cut out a shape from honeycomb candy. What seems simple becomes dangerous, especially for those with tricky shapes like the umbrella. One wrong move could cost them their life.

Episode 4: Stick to the Team

The players are split into teams for a brutal game of tug of war. They must rely on strength, teamwork, and strategy to survive. The stakes are high, and one wrong move could mean falling to their death.

Episode 5: A Fair World

The players face a new challenge that tests their ability to work with others. The game seems to offer a fair chance, but not everything is as it seems. Trust and betrayal come into play, and the tension rises even higher.

Episode 6: Gganbu

The players are paired up for a game that tests their trust and loyalty. As they face the challenge, they realize how far they’re willing to go for survival. The emotional stakes are high, and betrayal hits hard.

Episode 7: VIPs

The mysterious VIPs arrive, watching the games from a distance and betting on the players’ fates. The tension grows as the players are pushed to their limits. The stakes get even higher, and the cruelty of the games is laid bare.

Episode 8: Front Man

The true identity of the Front Man is revealed, adding another layer of mystery. As the final games approach, alliances are tested, and the players realize they’re not just fighting each other, but the system itself. The pressure to survive grows even more intense.

Episode 9: One Lucky Day

The final game begins, and the remaining players must face the ultimate challenge. With everything on the line, they fight to the death, each hoping for one lucky chance to survive. The shocking conclusion reveals the true cost of the game.
